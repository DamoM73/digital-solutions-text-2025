# Evaluation Table

Steps:

1. Create a evaluation table for the success criteria you established in the criteria table.
2. Copy the requirements and criteria established during the Explore phase into the table.
3. Establish a success rating for each criteria out of 5.
4. Justify the rating you have given for each criteria. You can use evidence from your testing, user feedback, and your own reflections to justify the ratings.
5. Provide recommendations for improving the application based on the ratings and justifications.
